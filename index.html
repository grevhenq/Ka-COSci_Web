CSSC Welfare Services

Ka-COSci Student Assistance Program (SAP)

The official portal of the College of Science Student Council to coordinate, evaluate, and provide aid across basic and specialized welfare sectors.

Available Assistance Categories

Every student of the College of Science is entitled to request support under the following council welfare channels.

In-Kind
Borrowing of Equipments

Temporary loan services for essential council equipments and materials.

In-Kind
Free Printing

Document and reviewer printing support for academic submissions.

In-Kind
Free Test Booklets

Provided test booklets for exams, evaluations, and department tests.

In-Kind
Free Coffee and Snacks

Refreshments at the CSSC office to support you during study sessions.

Financial
Emergency Allowance

Immediate monetary support for personal emergencies or sudden hardships.

Financial
Emergency Transportation

Transportation allowances for sudden emergency travel or medical transfers.

Financial
Emergency Food Subsidies

Food allowances for students dealing with sudden economic difficulty.

Financial
Lodging/Dorm Assistance

Subsidies to offset urgent dormitory dues or lodging emergencies.

Financial
Inpatient Hospitalization Assistance

Financial help for hospital stays, surgeries, or inpatient clinical charges.

Financial
Outpatient Hospitalization Assistance

Support for clinic check-ups, diagnostic lab tests, or medicines.

Financial
Legal Fee Assistance

Subsidies to offset legal consultations or structural representation.

Financial
Psych Fee Assistance

Financial coverage for clinical psychological counsel or treatments.

Financial
Cancer Therapy Assistance

Grants to support students undergoing cancer treatment or chemotherapy.

Financial
Physical Therapy Assistance

Subsidy for recovering students who require physical therapy or rehab.

Financial
Disability Assistance

Modifiers, aids, and services for differently-abled COS students.

Financial
Burial Assistance

Supportive funding allocated for students in unfortunate events.

Financial
Board Exam Review Assistance

Funding linkage, review materials, and licensure fee subsidies.

Financial
Project Support Grants for Student Orgs

Grants for student-led activities and recognized organizations.

Financial
Academic Tutorial Support for Student Orgs

Financial help for organizing student review classes or peer tutorials.
