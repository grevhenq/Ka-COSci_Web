CSSC Welfare Services

Ka-COSci Student Assistance Program (SAP)

The official portal of the College of Science Student Council to coordinate, evaluate, and provide aid across basic and specialized welfare sectors.

Available Assistance Categories

Every student of the College of Science is entitled to request support under the following council welfare channels.

CSSC Borrowing of Materials

Temporary loan services for essential science equipment, lab tools, and council materials.

In-Patient Hospitalization

Financial medical grants designed to cushion charges for hospital stays and clinical surgeries.

Out-Patient Hospitalization

Assistance for immediate clinic consultations, lab tests, medications, and minor check-ups.

Legal Assistance

Direct linkage and supportive structural representation on legal concerns within campus parameters.

Psych Assistance

Compassionate linkage and financial endorsement to mental health counsel and services.

Physical Therapy

Subsidy for recovering students who require physical therapy or rehabilitative treatments.

Disablement Assistance

Tailored campus modifications, aids, and services for differently-abled CoSci students.

Burial Assistance

Supportive funding allocated for students coping with the death of immediate family members.

Emergency Allowance

Immediate monetary help for unforeseen disasters, fires, or natural crisis scenarios.

Board Exam Review Aid

Page support, review resources, and registration fee subsidies for student board examinees.

Project Support Grants

Financial help for official programs arranged by science department councils or recognized student bodies.

Tutorial Support

Assistance in funding peer tutoring networks and study resources for department organizations.
